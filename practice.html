Practice

Filter and try practices for mind, body, and soul

🧠 Mind Practice: Gratitude Reflection

Each morning, write down three things you’re grateful for to start your day with positivity and awareness.

🧠 Mind Practice: Mindful Breathing

Take five slow, deep breaths, paying close attention to the sensations of breathing in and out.

🧠 Mind Practice: Focus Exercise

Spend 2 minutes observing an object (like a candle or plant) and gently return your attention if your mind wanders.

🏃‍♂️ Body Practice: Mindful Stretching

Spend 5 minutes doing gentle stretches, focusing on your breath and the sensations in your body.

🏃‍♂️ Body Practice: Nature Walk

Take a 10-minute walk outdoors, noticing the sights, sounds, and smells around you.

🏃‍♂️ Body Practice: Hydration Check

Drink a glass of water slowly, being fully present with the act of drinking.

✨ Soul Practice: Loving-Kindness Meditation

Take a few moments to send kind thoughts to yourself and others, cultivating compassion and inner peace.

✨ Soul Practice: Silent Reflection

Sit in silence for 3 minutes, observing thoughts without judgment and connecting with your inner self.

✨ Soul Practice: Acts of Service

Do a small act of kindness for someone else today, such as offering a compliment or helping a friend.
